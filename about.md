<h1>About This Blog</h1>
<p>Welcome to <strong>Daily Catholic Reflection</strong>, a space for contemplation on faith, scripture, and daily life through the lens of Catholic tradition.</p>

<h2>What You'll Find Here</h2>
<ul>
    <li>📖 <strong>Daily Scripture Reflections</strong> - Insights on Mass readings and liturgical seasons</li>
    <li>🙏 <strong>Prayer & Devotion</strong> - Traditional and contemporary Catholic prayers</li>
    <li>✝️ <strong>Lenten Journey</strong> - Special focus during Lent on prayer, fasting, and almsgiving</li>
    <li>💬 <strong>Community Discussion</strong> - Comments enabled for shared reflection</li>
</ul>

<h2>My Faith Journey</h2>
<p>This blog is born from a desire to deepen my relationship with God through daily reflection and to share that journey with others. Each post is an invitation to pause, pray, and grow together in faith.</p>

<h2>Comments</h2>
<p>All posts have comments enabled via <strong>giscus</strong> (GitHub-based). You'll need a GitHub account to comment, but this ensures a spam-free, thoughtful discussion environment.</p>
